Features
Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.
Built for Mac from the ground up — native performance, native keyboard shortcuts, native everything.
📊

Smart Dashboard

See Active, Overdue, Due Today, and Done Today at a single glance every time you open the app.

✍️

Rich Descriptions

Bold text, bullet lists, interactive checklists, and tables. Your tasks deserve more than a one-liner.

🔁

Recurring Tasks

Set tasks to repeat daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly. Next occurrence is created automatically on completion.

📎

Comments & Attachments

Add comments, attach files, images, and PDFs. All context lives inside the task where it belongs.

☁️

iCloud Sync

Tasks sync automatically across all your Apple devices via your private iCloud account.

🕐

Full History

Every status and priority change is automatically logged with a timestamp. Always know how a task evolved.

📅

Calendar View

Visualize your tasks by due date in a monthly calendar. Never lose track of what's coming up.

🔔

Smart Notifications

Get reminded about overdue tasks, tasks due today, and tasks due soon — at a time that suits you.

📄

Export to PDF

Export any task as a beautifully formatted PDF. Choose what to include — description, comments, history.

About
Built by one person,
for real daily use.

It's been refined through real daily use and is now available for anyone who feels the same way. No corporate backing, no VC funding — just a tool built with care by someone who uses it every single day.
